Rules of the game

Your mission is to skillfully collect eggs by mouse-clicking on them. But beware dear hunter, some of these eggs house unhatched dragon babies. Their mom is waiting to catch you off guard!
Pay close attention to the numbered clues appearing on certain eggs you have clicked. These hints will reveal how many dragon eggs are lurking directly around your chosen egg. By strategically deciphering these clues, you can unlock the path to victory by safely uncovering all the non-dragon eggs.
As an esteemed Egg Hunter, you possess a remarkable tool in your arsenal: the mighty "Mark" button. When you click on this button, you enter the marking mode, enabling you to exercise your keen judgment. With a mere click, you can mark an egg as a potential dragon egg without collecting it. This clever tactic allows you to carefully assess the eggs, distinguishing between the treasured and the treacherous. Should you change your mind, a simple click (while in marking mode) on the marked egg will make it available for collecting once again.
Embrace the power of the "Mark" button, and let your strategic prowess guide you towards triumph in this exhilarating egg-hunting adventure!

Exercise caution, embrace strategy, and may your egg-hunting skills lead you to triumph!
