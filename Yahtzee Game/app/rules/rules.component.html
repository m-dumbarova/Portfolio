YAHTZEE - game rules

Object of the Game

Roll dice to get the highest score after all 13 rounds.

Rules in short

  • Roll up to 3 times each turn to rack up the best possible score.
  • Decide which dice combo you're going for.
  • After each turn, write your score in one empty box on the score card.

Scoring

There are 13 scoring boxes. On each completed turn, the player must score in one of the 13 boxes. The boxes may be filled in any order, according to the player's best judgment. The score card has an upper section and a lower section:

UPPER SECTION

Aces - Sum all the Aces that you've rolled. = 1 point
Twos - Sum all the Twos that you've rolled. = 2 point
Threes - Sum all the Threes that you've rolled. = 3 point
Fours - Sum all the Fours that you've rolled. = 4 point
Fives - Sum all the Fives that you've rolled. = 5 point
Sixes - Sum all the Sixes that you've rolled. = 6 point

LOWER SECTION

3 of a kind
If you have 3 of a kind, sum all these dice.
Example: 3*6 + 3 + 5 = 26 points
Total: 26 points
4 of a kind
If you have 4 of a kind, sum all these dice.
Example: 4*5 + 2 = 22 points
Total: 22 points
Full House
3 of a kind and 2 of another kind.
Full House is always worth 25 points!
Example: 3*3 + 2*5 = 19 points, but you receive 25 points.
Total: 25 points
Small Straight
Any sequence of 4 numbers.
Any Small Straight is always worth 30 points!
Example: 1 + 2 + 3 + 4 + 2 = 12 points, but you receive 30 points.
Total: 30 points
Large Straight
Any sequence of 5 numbers.
Any Large Straight is always worth 40 points!
Example: 1 + 2 + 3 + 4 + 5 = 15 points, but you receive 40 points.
Total: 40 points
YAHTZEE
5 of a kind.
The first YAHTZEE you score is always worth 50 points!
But notice! - You may score again YAHTZEE and then you receive 100 points bonus.
Example: 2 + 2 + 2 + 2 + 2 = 10 points, but you receive 50 points.
Total: 50 points
Chance
Score the total of any 5 dice.
Example: 1 + 2 + 2 + 5 + 3 = 13 points
Total: 13 points
